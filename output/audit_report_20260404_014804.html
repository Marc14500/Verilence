Conflicting Basis for Operator's Revenue Override Calculation

What We Found

Section 4.4: From Net Revenue, the following allocations are made: ... (B) Operator's Revenue Override: 2.5% of GROSS revenue (before deductions)... vs Section 4.3: [Describes a waterfall of deductions from Gross Revenue to arrive at a final] 'Net Revenue to Joint Account' Problem: This is a direct mathematical and procedural contradiction. A value (the 2.5% override) calculated from Gross Revenue cannot logically be allocated *from* a smaller, subsequent value (Net Revenue). Ambiguous and Contradictory 'Sole Risk' Definition

What We Found

Problem: The 'Sole Risk' definition suggests the Operator can unilaterally drill wells for its own account, completely bypassing the joint approval and cost-sharing structure mandated by the AFE process in Section 2.4.
