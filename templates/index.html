Explainable Boosting Machine (EBM) & Auditability

What Is an Explainable Boosting Machine?

An Explainable Boosting Machine (EBM) is a machine learning model that combines the power of sophisticated AI with complete transparency. Unlike "black box" AI models that make decisions in ways no one can understand, an EBM shows you exactly how it arrived at every conclusion.

Think of it like this:

Black Box AI: "Trust me, this is a contradiction" (you have no idea why)

EBM: "This is a contradiction because: Signal A shows 80%, Signal B shows 90%, Signal C shows 75%. Combined: 85% confidence. Here's the formula." (complete transparency)

Why Auditability Matters for Legal Documents

In legal work, you can't just trust a tool. You need to understand it. You need to defend it. You need to explain it to opposing counsel, regulators, or a judge.

  • Court Defense: If a contradiction we found ends up in litigation, you need to explain how we found it. With EBM, you can show the exact calculation.
  • Regulatory Compliance: SOX, FCPA, and other regulations require audit trails. Our EBM provides a complete, reproducible audit trail for every finding.
  • Professional Liability: If someone challenges your analysis, you need mathematical proof, not guessing. EBM gives you that proof.
  • Cross-Examination: Opposing counsel will ask "How did you find that?" With EBM, you can answer with math, not opinion.

How Verilence Uses EBM for Auditability

1. Transparent Confidence Scoring

Every finding gets a confidence score (0-100%) based on three measurable signals:

  • Text Clarity (35%): How obvious is the contradiction in the document?
  • AI Reliability (35%): How reliable is Google Gemini at this task?
  • Chunk Quality (30%): Did we pull the right document sections?

Result: You can see exactly why we're confident (or not) in each finding.

2. Mathematical Proof

Every number is calculated using a formula you can verify:

Confidence = (0.35 × Risk Certainty) + (0.35 × EBM Confidence) + (0.30 × Retrieval Score)

Result: No black box. No guessing. Pure math.

3. Complete Audit Trail

For each contradiction we find, you get:

  • Exact document sections involved
  • How each signal was calculated
  • Why confidence is what it is
  • Routing decision (analyst review, partner escalation, etc.)

Result: Complete documentation. Ready for audit or court.

4. Reproducibility

Run the same analysis on the same document 100 times. You'll get the exact same results every time. No randomness. No surprises.

Result: You can defend your findings with confidence.

Why This Matters vs. Other AI Tools

Typical AI Legal Tools: "We found a contradiction" (no explanation, black box)

Verilence: "We found a contradiction with 85% confidence because: [exact calculation shown]. Here's why. Here's the math. Here's the audit trail."

The Glass Box Standard

We call this the "Glass Box" approach—everything is visible, transparent, and auditable. You can see through it to the logic underneath.

Glass Box vs. Black Box:
